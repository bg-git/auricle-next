## 2. HOW TO RAISE A COMPLAINT (B2B)

We aim to resolve issues in a clear and fair way.

### Step 1: Contact us  
Email **info@auricle.co.uk** and include the following:

• Order number, if relevant  
• Your business name and contact information  
• A clear and factual summary of the issue  
• Any supporting documents or images (such as delivery packaging photos or product images)

### Step 2: Escalation  
If the initial reply does not resolve your concern, reply to the same email and request an **escalation**. A senior reviewer will reassess the case.

### Step 3: Final review  
If the issue remains unresolved, request a **final review**. We will then set out our final position.

**Target response times:** We aim to acknowledge within **five business days**.  
Cases involving carriers or suppliers may require more time due to investigations outside our control.

Additional resources:  
• Issues relating to faulty goods or delivery should follow the timeframes in the [RETURNS AND FAULTY GOODS POLICY](/information/returns-and-faulty-goods-policy).  
• Issues relating to shipping should follow the [SHIPPING AND DELIVERY POLICY](/information/shipping-and-delivery-policy).  
• Intellectual property concerns should follow [DESIGNS & IP](/information/design-copyright-ip-notice).  
• Concerns relating to modern slavery should follow the [MODERN SLAVERY STATEMENT](/information/modern-slavery-statement).

## 3. FORMAL LEGAL NOTICES TO AURICLE

Formal notices include matters such as contract notices, claim letters or statutory notices.  
You may use **either** of the following channels.

### 3.1 Email for legal notices

**Address:** **info@auricle.co.uk**  
**Subject format:** `LEGAL NOTICE | [Your Company] | [Short Summary]`  
**Deemed receipt:** 24 hours after the email is sent, unless you receive a bounce message or an invalid-address warning.

### 3.2 Registered post or courier

Send to:

**For the attention of: Company Secretary | LEGAL NOTICE**  
AURICLE Limited  
8 Silver Walk  
Leicester  
LE1 5EW  
England

**Deemed receipt for UK post:** Three days after posting by first-class mail, or the next business day after recorded courier delivery.  
**Deemed receipt for non-UK post:** On confirmed delivery, or seven days after dispatch, whichever occurs first.

Keep your proof of posting or delivery.  
Notices sent to any other address, including the PO Box, may be delayed or considered not received.

## 4. NOTICES FROM AURICLE TO YOU

We may send notices to the email address or postal address you provided when creating your account or placing an order.

• Email notices are deemed received 24 hours after sending unless undeliverable.  
• First-class UK post is deemed received three days after posting.

Ensure your contact details remain accurate.

---

## 5. WHAT TO INCLUDE IN A FORMAL NOTICE

A complete notice should contain:

• Your legal name, registered number and address  
• The basis for the notice (contract, claim, statute or other)  
• A factual summary and the remedy requested  
• Relevant references such as order numbers or invoice numbers  
• Evidence, such as photos or attached documents  
• A clear and reasonable response deadline

---

## 6. CHANNELS WE DO NOT ACCEPT FOR NOTICES

We do not accept legal notices through:

• Social platforms  
• Live chat, including ChatGPT-powered chat  
• SMS or messaging apps  
• Phone calls

These channels may be used for general enquiries only.
