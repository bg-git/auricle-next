**Effective Date:** 9 August 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how AURICLE Limited (“AURICLE”, “we”, “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies on [www.auricle.co.uk](https://www.auricle.co.uk) and [www.auriclejewelry.com](https://www.auriclejewelry.com).  
For information about how we handle personal data, refer to the [Privacy Policy](/information/privacy-policy).

> **Consent model:** These cookies are essential to enable core website functions such as account access, security, and checkout. Without these cookies, the website cannot operate correctly. We do not offer per-category cookie settings. You may accept cookies and use the Website, or decline and not use the Website. You may also block cookies in your browser, but the Website and checkout may not function correctly if you do so.

---

## 1) WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.  
We also use local storage and similar technologies that perform comparable functions.

---

## 2) WHAT WE USE COOKIES FOR

We use cookies and similar technologies that are essential for operating the Website and delivering the service. These include:

• Session and authentication (keeping you signed in and maintaining cart and checkout state)  
• Security and fraud prevention (protecting accounts and preventing abuse)  
• Availability and performance (load balancing and basic error diagnostics)  
• Regional settings (remembering region and currency needed for pricing and fulfilment)

We do not offer granular controls for optional categories.  
If you do not accept cookie use, please do not use the Website.

**Shopify Checkout:** When checkout takes place on Shopify domains, Shopify may set cookies that are strictly necessary for operating checkout and preventing fraud. These cookies are controlled by Shopify.

---

## 3) EXAMPLES OF COOKIES AND STORAGE

Actual items may change as we update the Website or our providers.

| Cookie / Storage | Provider | Purpose | Type | Expiry |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `__host_session` (example) | Site | Secure session and essential features | First-party | Session |
| `region` / `currency` | Site | Region and currency needed for pricing | First-party | 6 months |
| `cart` / `checkout` | Site and checkout | Cart persistence and checkout flow | First-party and third-party | Up to 30 days |
| `error_log_id` | Site | Diagnostics for availability | First-party | 30 days |
| `localStorage:favourites` | Site | Saved items via local storage | First-party | Until removed |

## 4) MANAGING COOKIES

• We do not offer category-level cookie choices. To use the Website, you must accept cookies. If you do not accept, do not use the Website.  
• You may block or delete cookies through your browser settings. If you do so, essential functions such as login, cart and checkout will not operate.
