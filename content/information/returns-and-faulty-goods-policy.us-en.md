## 1. SCOPE

This policy applies to B2B purchases from AURICLE.  
Website use is governed by the [TERMS OF SERVICE](/information/terms-of-service).

---

## 2. SUMMARY

• Returns accepted only for confirmed faults  
• No returns for change of mind, over ordering or compatibility issues  
• Reporting timelines are listed in section 3  
• Remedies include repair, like for like replacement or a credit note if neither is available (see section 6)

---

## 3. REPORTING TIMEFRAMES

**Delivery damage, shortages or picking errors**  
• Report within **24 hours of delivery** (UK time) to **info@auricle.co.uk**  
• Include order number, clear photos of packaging and items and a description of the issue

**Latent manufacturing defects (not visible on delivery)**  
• Report within **7 calendar days of discovery**  
• Must be reported no later than **30 days from delivery**

This window exists to allow reasonable inspection for issues that could not be identified at the moment of delivery.

Claims reported outside these timeframes may be declined.

---

## 4. ELIGIBILITY

A return will be authorised only where we confirm a manufacturing fault or other non conformity.  
The following do not qualify as faults:

• Normal wear and tear  
• Improper care or misuse  
• Modification or unauthorised repairs  
• Damage occurring after delivery

---

## 5. RETURNS AUTHORISATION (RMA)

• You must obtain a **Return Merchandise Authorisation (RMA)** before sending any item back  
• Items must be returned complete, safely packaged and marked with the RMA number on the outer box  
• Unauthorised returns may be refused or returned to sender

---

## 6. REMEDIES

Where a fault is confirmed, we will choose one of the following:

1. Repair the item  
2. Replace the item with a like for like equivalent  
3. If repair or replacement is not possible within a reasonable time, issue a **credit note** for the invoiced value of the faulty item

Refunds are issued only where legally required.

---

## 7. COSTS AND RISK IN RETURN SHIPPING

For authorised faulty returns, we will provide a prepaid label or reimburse standard return costs.  
You remain responsible for the goods until they arrive with us.  
Tracked or insured shipping is recommended.

---

## 8. HOW TO REPORT

Email **info@auricle.co.uk** with:

• Order number and date  
• Affected SKU codes and quantities  
• Clear photos or videos of the issue and the packaging  
• A brief description of the defect and when it was discovered
