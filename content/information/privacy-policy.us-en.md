## 3) Data we collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

• **Account and identity data:** name, business name, job title or role, email, phone number and password hash  
• **Business details and addresses:** billing and shipping information, company identifiers such as VAT, preferred region and currency  
• **Order and support data:** orders, invoices, returns and communication with our team  
• **Usage and device information:** IP address, browser and device details, pages viewed, referral source and approximate location as inferred from IP  
• **Marketing preferences:** newsletter sign up, opt in or opt out  
• **Cookies and similar technologies:** see the [Cookie Policy](/information/cookie-policy)

We do not store full payment card numbers. Payment data is handled securely by our checkout and payment providers.

---

## 4) How we collect data

• **Directly from you:** when you register an account, place an order or contact us  
• **Automatically:** through cookies, logs and analytics when you browse the Website  
• **From third parties:** including address verification services, fraud prevention providers, courier integrations and permitted business data sources

## 5) Purposes and lawful bases

| Purpose | Examples | Lawful basis |
|---|---|---|
| Account setup and management | Approving wholesale accounts, authentication, account contact | **Contract** and **Legitimate interests** (B2B operation and security) |
| Order processing and delivery | Taking payment, fulfilling orders, handling returns, issuing invoices | **Contract** and **Legal obligation** (tax and records) |
| Support and communication | Responding to enquiries, providing service updates | **Legitimate interests** (operation and support) |
| Security and fraud prevention | Access controls, monitoring for suspicious activity | **Legitimate interests** and **Legal obligation** |
| Analytics and performance | Measuring traffic and improving navigation | **Legitimate interests** and **Consent** where required (cookies) |
| Marketing | Email updates relating to products or services | **Consent**, or **Legitimate interests** with opt out where permitted |

Marketing consent may be withdrawn at any time using the unsubscribe link or by contacting us.

We share personal data with:

• E commerce and payment providers, including Shopify checkout and your chosen payment processor

• IT and cloud service providers that support hosting, email, analytics and security  
• Professional advisers such as legal and accounting services

## 7) International transfers

Some providers may process data outside the UK or EEA.  
Where this occurs, we apply appropriate safeguards such as adequacy decisions or standard contractual clauses, in line with UK and EU data protection law.

## 8) Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described:

• **Accounts and orders:** retained during the business relationship and normally for six years after the end of the financial year for tax and record keeping  
• **Support correspondence:** up to twenty four months after resolution  
• **Marketing data:** until you withdraw consent or object, or after an extended period of inactivity  
• **Logs and analytics:** generally held for twelve to twenty four months, and anonymised earlier where possible

We may retain data longer where required by law or to establish, exercise or defend legal claims.

## 9) Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may exercise the following rights:

• **Access** to your personal data  
• **Rectification** of inaccurate or incomplete data  
• **Erasure** in certain circumstances  
• **Restriction** or **objection** to processing  
• **Portability** in a structured and commonly used format  
• **Withdrawal of consent** for processing based on consent  
• Freedom from decisions based solely on **automated processing** that produce legal or similarly significant effects

To exercise your rights, email **info@auricle.co.uk**.  
You may also raise concerns with the **UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO)** or your local data authority in the EEA.
