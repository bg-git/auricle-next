> **MANDATORY US IMPORT TERM:** For shipments entering the United States, duties and taxes must be paid in advance through our checkout system. Parcels cannot enter the United States until duties are paid.

---

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to orders shipped to addresses in the United States.  
Website use is governed by the [TERMS OF SERVICE](/information/terms-of-service).

---

## 2. Carrier

• All US orders are shipped with **DHL Express**

Available services may vary depending on address verification, order value and security checks.

---

## 3. Charges, estimates and timing

• Shipping charges are displayed at checkout  
• Delivery times are estimates only and depend on DHL performance and customs processing  
• Timeframes apply from dispatch, not from the time the order is placed

---

## 4. Dispatch and handling

• Orders are dispatched on business days (excluding UK public holidays)  
• Payment verification and anti fraud checks may affect dispatch time  
• Orders may be combined or shipped in parts depending on logistics

---

## 5. Addresses and delivery requirements

• We ship only to valid US addresses  
• PO Boxes are not accepted  
• A signature may be required  
• Customers must provide complete and accurate address details at checkout

---

## 6. Tracking and delivery issues

• Tracking is provided once the order is dispatched  
• If tracking shows delivered but the parcel is missing, contact DHL first, then notify us  
• Report issues quickly so we can support the DHL investigation

---

## 7. Duties, taxes and customs (United States)

**US duty rule:**  
• Duties and taxes must be paid **in advance**  
• These charges will appear at checkout  
• Parcels cannot clear US Customs unless duties are paid before arrival

Further points:

• Import duties, taxes and brokerage charges are your responsibility  
• US Customs may delay shipments for inspection  
• If duties are unpaid or the recipient cannot be contacted, the shipment may be returned or destroyed by DHL  
• Any return, storage or handling charges resulting from unpaid duties are your responsibility

---

## 8. Failed delivery and returned parcels

• If delivery fails due to address issues or unpaid duties, DHL may return the shipment  
• We may charge return and re shipping fees  
• Re shipping takes place once any outstanding fees are paid

---

## 9. Risk and title

• Risk transfers to you on delivery  
• Title transfers when full payment is received  
• See the [TERMS OF SALE](/information/terms-of-sale) for more information

---

## 10. Damage, loss and shortages

• Inspect your shipment at the time of receipt  
• Report visible damage or shortages to DHL and then inform us  
• Keep all packaging until the investigation is complete

For faults, refer to the [RETURNS POLICY](/information/returns-and-faulty-goods-policy).

---

## 11. Restrictions

• Certain items may be restricted by US law, including some materials or product categories  
• Orders that cannot enter the United States due to legal restrictions may be cancelled and refunded for the undeliverable items only

---

## 12. Force majeure

AURICLE is not responsible for delays caused by events outside our control, including weather, DHL disruption, customs action or government intervention.

---

Changes take effect once they appear on this page.
